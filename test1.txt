Social media is a popular tool for connecting with people, and advertising; however, the way it is set up and used has potential for a lot of privacy issues. When users use a social media platform, they are often exposing their private information. On the other hand, they can also find out others private information. Social media platforms try to protect users shared information using encryption techniques.
